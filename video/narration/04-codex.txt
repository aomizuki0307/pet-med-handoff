Codex with GPT five point six helped me trace two failures that unit tests alone did not expose: production Firebase configuration leaking into the offline mock build, and WorkManager initialization crashing at cold start. I made the product and safety decisions, then used Codex to implement the pure Kotlin handoff model, Compose flow, and regression tests. The result is a public, MIT-licensed repository with twenty-two passing tests and an account-free judge APK.
